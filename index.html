Creamy Garlic Spaghetti

Ingredients
  • 200g spaghetti.
  • 2 cloves garlic (minced).
  • 1/2 cup cream.
  • 2 tbsp olove oil.
  • satl & pepper to taste.
  • fresh parsley (optional).
Instructions
  1. Boil pasta in salted water al dente.
  2. Saute garlic in olive oil for 1-2 minute.
  3. Add cream and stir over low heat.
  4. Drain pasta and mix into sauce.
  5. Season with salt,pepper and parley serve hot!.
